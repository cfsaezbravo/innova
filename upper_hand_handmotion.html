HAND MOTION


INDICATIONS

The implants of the HandMotion range are intended for the fixation of bone fractures and osteotomies and for arthrodeses of hand in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Cannulated, self-tapping, self-compressive screws

Cylindro-conical shape head for non-traumatic insertion

Short and long-threaded screws depending on the location of the fracture line

Ergonomically designed instruments featuring three functions (screw-driver, countersink and depth gauge), available in the three diameters of cannulated screws for reducing operative time


IMPLANTS

Short-threaded cannulated screws in 3 diameters: Ø3.0 mm, Ø2.6 mm and Ø2.25 mm, length 14 to 34 mm (2 mm increment)

Long-threaded cannulated screws in 3 diameters: Ø3.0 mm, Ø2.6 mm and Ø2.25 mm, length 10 to 34 mm (2 mm increment)
