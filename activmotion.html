ACTIVMOTION LIGAMENTO

HTO + LCA


INDICATIONS

The implants of the Activ range are intended for high tibial osteotomy in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Implant well-suited for sports medicine: two-in-one opertaion for shortening patients rehabilitation time.
Optimal design and positioning allowing for the drilling of the tunnel without conflict - Proximal curvature and metaphyseal slope from 8° to 12°
Dualtec System technology: 2 polyaxial epiphyseal hole allowing +/- 12.5° angulation (25° cone)


IMPLANTS

Opening wedge tibial osteotomy plate combined with ACL reconstructive surgery (74.1 mm) available in right and left versions
Single fixation: 4.5 mm locking screws, length 30 to 75 mm (5mm increment)
