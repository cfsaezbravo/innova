ALIANS CLAVICLE


INDICATIONS

The implants of the Alians Clavicle range are dedicated to the fixation of fractures, mal-unions, non-unions and osteotomies of the clavicle in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Comprehensive range of plates: medial or lateral, superior or anterior, bendable or non-bendable plates

Locking precontoured plates

Dualtec System® 2 technology: polyaxial holes allowing screw angulation of +/- 10º (20º cone)


IMPLANTS

Superior non-bendable plates: medial in 2 sizes (69 & 89mm) or lateral in 2 sizes (75 & 96mm), available in right and left versions

-Superior bendable plates: medial in 2 sizes (83 & 125mm) or lateral (143mm) available in right and left versions

1 bendable anterio-medial plate (95mm)

Diaphyeal fixation: Ø3.5mm locking and non-locking cortical screws, length 10 to 24 mm (2mm increment)

Epiphyseal fixation: Ø2.8mm locking screws, length 10 to 24mm (2mm increment)
