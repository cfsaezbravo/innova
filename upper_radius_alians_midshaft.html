ALIANS MIDSHAFT


INDICATIONS

The implants of the Alians Forearm range are indended for the fixation of midshaft fractures of the radius and the ulna and ulnar shortening osteotomies in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Several possible approaches: palmar, lateral or dorsal

Cylindro-conical shape head for non-traumatic insertion

Low-profile plate with rounded edges to prevent soft tissue irritation

A range of 4 locking plates


IMPLANTS

Symmetrical locking plates coming in 4 sizes (length 80 to 160mm)

Single fixation: Ø3.5mm locking and non-locking cortical screws, length 10 to 26mm (2mm increment)
