ALIANS CLAVICLE

INDICATIONS

The implants of the Alians Proximal Humerus range are intended for osteosynthesis of fractures and fractures dislocations, osteotomies and non-unions of the proximal humerus in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Unique & easily accesible suture holes for soft tissue fixation
Ideal placement 1.5cm from the proximal edge of the greater tuberosity of the humerus and alongside the bicipital groove
DualTec System® technology: polyaxial holes allowing +/- 12.5º screw angulation (25º cone)
