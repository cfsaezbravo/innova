ALIANS ELBOW

Distal Humerus - Olecranon


INDICATIONS

The implants of the Alians Elbow range are dedicated to the fixation of fractures and osteotomies of the distal humerus and proximal ulna in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Precontoured implant including bendable sections

Dual System® 2 technology: polyaxialholes allowing +/- 10° screw angulation (20° cone) ensuring optimum flexibility to capture bone fragments and prevent screws impinging on each other Distal Humerus


Fixation with two plates:
Perpendicular fixation: medial plate ans posterol-lateral plate with or without a backing device
Pre-mounted lateral support tat can be removed in case of small size humerus or fractures of the capitellum

Fixation with one plate:
Y plate
Olecranon
60mm (5mm increment)

Narrow, low-profile implant minimizing risks of skin necrois and irritation of soft-tissues
Single screw diameter: 2.8mm
2 epiphyseal hooks making fractures reduction easier and improving implant stability
