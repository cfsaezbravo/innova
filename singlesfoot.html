INITIAL S™ FOOT

INDICATIONS

The implants of the Initial S™ range are intended for the fixation of bone fractures and osteotomies and for arthrodeses of foot in adults.

KIT CONTENT
  • Instrumentation only
  • Implants: Additional sterile screws can be ordered separated in sterile packaging.
IMPLANTS

Initial S™ Foot kits are available in version:

Plates:

2 types of plates:

Standard plates: Size 1 (75mm), Size 2 (97 mm) and Size 3 (128 mm), available in right and left version.

  • Ø2.25 mm instrumentation dedicated to the Ø2.25 mm cannulated self-drilling, self-tapping, self-compressive screws
    Long thread: from 8 to 34 mm (2 mm increments),
    Short thread: from 14 to 34 mm (2 mm increments).

  • Ø2.6 mm instrumentation dedicated to the Ø2.6 mm cannulated self-drilling, self-tapping, self-compressive screws:
    Long thread: from 10 to 34 mm (2 mm increments)
    Short thread: from 14 mm to 34 mm (2 mm increments).

  • Ø3.0 mm instrumentation dedicated to the Ø3.0 mm cannulated self-drilling, self-tapping, self-compressive screws:
    Long thread: from 10 to 40 mm (2 mm increments)
    Short thread: from 14 mm to 34 mm (2 mm increments).
