INITIAL A™

INDICATIONS

The implants of the Initial A™ range are intended for the fixation of fractures, osteotomies and pseudarthroses of the distal fibula, and for syndesmotic repair in adults.

KIT CONTENT
  • Instrumentation
  • Implants: plates and screws
IMPLANTS

Initial A kits are available with the following implants:

Plates:

2 types of plates:

Standard plates: Size 1 (75mm), Size 2 (97 mm) and Size 3 (128 mm), available in right and left version.

Narrow plates: Size 1 (76 mm) and size 2 (102 mm)

Technical features:

Precontoured implant,
Optimized design limiting risks of soft tissues irritations thans to the rounded distal edges of the plates

Dualtec System 2® technology: polyaxical holes allowing +/- 10 angulation (20° cone).

Fixation

  • Ø2.8 mm locking screws
  • Ø3.5 mm locking screws
  • Ø3.5 mm cortical screws

(Additional sterile screws can be ordered separated in Supplemental Sterile Screw Caddy)
