FOOTMOTION

Plating System - MTP

INDICATIONS

The Footmotion Plating System is intended for arthrodesis, fractures and osteotomies fixation and revision surgeries of the foot in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Range of Precontoured and low profile plates

Fixation and screws optimized (a single screw diameter, screw head minimally invasive and buried in the plate, hexaglobe screw recess design)

Specific fixation for optimal stability of the assembly (ramp oblong hole, hole for transfixation screw, holes or converging screws in the distal and proximal areas)


IMPLANTS

3 sizes of first metatarso-phalangeal arthrodesis plate available in right and left versions

Single fixation: Ø2.8mm locking and non-locking screws, length 10 to 34mm (2mm increment)
