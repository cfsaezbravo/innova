ALIANS ULNA 3/3


INDICATIONS

The implants of the Alians Radius range are intended for the fixation of extra and intro-articular fractures as well as distal radius osteotomies in adults.


TECHNICAL FEATURES

Comprehensive range of plates: narrow, standard and wide head

Palmar plate running alongside the watershed line

Optimal design reducing the risks of soft tissue irritation thanks to the rounded distal edges of the plates

Window in the metaphyso-epiphyseal area for better visualization of the fracture reduction

Dualtec System 2 technology: polyaxial holes allowing +/- 10 angulation (20° cone)


IMPLANTS

Narrow head plates available in 2 sizes, standard-head plates available in 3 sizes and wide head plates available in 1 size

Single fixation: Ø2.8mm locking screws, length 10 to 28mm (2mm increment), non-locking screws, length 10 to 28mm (2mm increment) and non-locking cortical screws, length 10 to 18mm (2mm increment).
